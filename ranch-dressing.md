# Ranch dressing

## Summary

- Servings: 1/2 cup
- Prep: 5 mins
- Cook: 0 mins
- Inactive: 0 mins
- Total: 5 mins

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup mayo
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp corriander
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- salt/pepper to taste

## Directions

1. combine all ingredients
