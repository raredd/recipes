# Pancakes

## Summary

- Servings: 4
- Prep: 10 mins
- Cook: 10 mins
- Inactive: 0 mins
- Total: 20 mins

## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 cup almond milk
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Directions

1. combine dry and wet ingredients separately; combine and whisk smooth
1. let batter rest for 5 min before cooking
